Harvard Model Congress Dubai
VGS Students @ the Harvard Model Congress Dubai

Harvard Model Congress Dubai, a non-profit organization which is run entirely by Harvard University students conducted an international student conference from 19th January to 21st January 2017 at the ‘American University in Dubai’. It provides students from across the globe a platform for an immersive educational experience. Throughout the intensive three-day conference, students worked closely with their committees to debate briefings written by Harvard students, drafted legislation and presented it in the full sessions. Students got an opportunity to present their ideas to their peers and learn to compromise to get their legislation to pass. On the second day of the conference, "Harvard-AUD College Panel" was organized to address the queries of students on the admission process of US universities. It helped students aspiring to get into US universities a better perspective towards the admission process. More than 375 delegates from different backgrounds, countries, and ways of life came together to debate, collaborate and learn in a dynamic atmosphere. Velammal Global School students had an exposure to global issues in a truly global conference and had helped them take the baby-steps to become a global citizens

ULead Student-Led Conference [20-21 Jan 2018]
ULead Student-Led Conference [20-21 Jan 2018]

Operation Smile is a non-profit organization based out of USA that works to provide free surgeries for cleft lip and palate patients all around the world who are unable to afford them. ULead @ American International School, is a conference which was established to foster leadership skills and raise awareness for ‘Operation smile’. Completely student-led, the two day workshop on leadership, volunteerism and awareness camps for medical missions was attended by our Grade 9 students who were al inspired to become ‘ambassadors of peace’ here in Chennai.The conference was to speak to and inspire persistence and resilience that comes with active service work.
